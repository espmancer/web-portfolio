Omi
Universe: Omi

Summary

"The Omi Universe", "The Omi", or just "Omi", is an encapsulation of species, planets, and languages created by me.

Magic

Magic comes from arcanons, an elementary particle produced by high energy reactions, such as galactic events, nuclear events, earthquakes, electrostatic discharge, and more. Arcanons affect the forces of the natural world by replicating their behavior or influencing them.

Effects

Effects are the forces being affected. There are 5 effects:

  • Temperature: The manipulation of molecular motion
  • Electromagnetism: The manipulation of the electromagnetic force
  • Gravity: The manipulation of gravitational fields
  • Sound: The manipulation of pressure waves
  • Nuclear: The manipulation of the strong and weak nuclear forces

Cuts and Shapes

Certain cuts of crystal will produce different effect shapes. Uncut crystals often produce irregular shapes.

Common Shapes
  • Cylindrical
  • Conical
  • Cubical
  • Spherical

Effect Tiers

Tiers are organizational labels that can be used to compare or understand the rough scale of an effect. They are determined by the level of non-magic technology required to produce the same effect. Effects are formatted as "Effect #" (e.g. Temperature 3).

  1. Tier 1: Produceable with household items
  2. Tier 2: Produceable with industrial equipment
  3. Tier 3: Produceable with specialized scientific equipment
  4. Tier 4: Produceable with complex theoretical technology
  5. Tier 5: Beyond technological reproduction

Crystals

Crystals, including some allotropes, are the most common way to harness arcanons.

Charging

Given that arcaonns come from high energy events, the easiest way to charge a crystal is to get lightning to strike it. The more charged a crystal is (i.e. the more arcanon-dense it is) the more unstable it gets. Instability comes in the form of vibration and higher likelyhood to break the crystal or release arcanons prematurely.

Release

Charged crystals normally can hold a certain amount of arcanons before they need to release those arcanons. However, crystals can also be primed with kinetic or sound energy to release arcanons. The amount of energy released is proportial to the amount of energy provided.

Release Rate

Different crystals release arcanons at different rates.

  • Immediate - The effect occurs now until all energy is transferred to magic
  • Flow - The effect occurs over a consistent, slower period until all energy is transferred to magic
  • Pulse - The effect occurs in short bursts until all energy is transferred to magic

Resonance Fusing

Crystals can be fused together given that they vibrate at a particular frequency with other crystals. Fused crystals can combine effects together, amplify existing effects, or create new effects altogether. This frequency has been observed to be the sum of every crystal's resonant frequency divided by e.

 (∑(hz))/e

Unfusing

Unfusing crystals requires vibrating at a significantly lower frequency, which has been observed to be the natural log of the original fusing frequency.

 ln(hz)
