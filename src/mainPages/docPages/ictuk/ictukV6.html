There are a total of 13 letters in Ictuk.

It's important to note that /kzh/ is not a sound humans are physically capable of making, but Ictuza are (because /k/ is made with their mandibles, and /zh/ is made with their wings), so when speaking in Ictuk, it is acceptable to say them in succession rather than at the same time.
