Summary

Vemachia sapiens ("smart wasp warrior") or V. sapiens are a species of humanoid wasps who share similar DNA to the warrior wasps found on Earth.

Anatomy

Head

In the daylight, V. sapiens can see between 300-480 nm of light, but at night, they can see between 600-800 nm. This is due to a liquid compound located in their eyes that contains proteins which bond with their cone cells. When cooled, this compound solidifies, keeping the proteins from bonding and shifting their vision.
V. sapiens have a proboscis to eat and speak. This is normally retracted, but muscles in the back of the mouth tighten to act like a tongue that can stick out.
