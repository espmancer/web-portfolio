Chapter 1: Survival Ictuk

These are basic words and sentences to learn for introducing yourself, in case you're in an Ictuza city.

Words to Remember
  • I: Tuf
  • You: But
  • Be: Kofa
  • Good: Fash
  • Bad: Ofash
  • Yes: Fash
  • No: O
  • Hello: Fotash
Phrases to Remember
  • I am good: Kofafashtuf
  • Are you good?: Kofafashbut?
  • I am bad: Kofaofashtuf
  • I am not good: Okofafashtuf
