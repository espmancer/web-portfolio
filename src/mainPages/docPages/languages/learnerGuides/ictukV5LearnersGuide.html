Chapter 1: Survival Ictuk

Chapter Goals

You will be able to:

  1. Introduce yourself in Ictuk
  2. Remember the pronunciation of letters in introductory words
  3. Hold a very basic, small-talk conversation in Ictuk

Words to Remember

  • I: Tuf
  • You: But
  • Be: Kofa
  • Good: Fash
  • Bad: Ofash
  • Yes: Fash
  • No: O
  • Hello: Fotash

Phrases to Remember

  • I am good: Kofafashtuf
  • Are you good?: Kofafashbut?
  • I am bad: Kofaofashtuf
  • I am not good: Okofafashtuf

Exercises

  1. What does this mean?
    Kofafashbut?


  2. How do you say this?
    Hello, I am good.


  3. What does this mean?
    O, okofafashtuf.
